The share performance of Pfeiffer Vacuum in the first three quarters of 2020 was slightly stronger than the performance of the TecDAX index during the same period. The opening price of Pfeiffer Vacuum shares was € 160.00 on January 2, 2020, and the closing price was € 177.00 on September 30, 2020, which represents an increase of 10.6%. The high for the first nine months of 2020 was € 180.60 on July 22, 2020, and the low was € 104.40 on March 16, 2020. In comparison, the TecDAX index increased by 1.6% from 3,023 points on January 2, 2020, to 3,070 points on September 30, 2020.
