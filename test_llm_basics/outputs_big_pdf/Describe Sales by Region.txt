Sales by region for Pfeiffer Vacuum Group are broken down as follows:

- France contributed 6.6% of total sales, which is a decrease from the previous 9.5%.
- Germany accounted for 15.6% of sales, down from the previous 17.6%.
- The category "All Others" represented 3.3% of sales, a decrease from the previous 4.3%.
- The "Rest of Asia" made a significant contribution with 18.6% of sales, up from the previous 16.0%.
- "Rest of Europe" contributed 16.7% to the total sales, slightly down from 17.1%.
- The USA was a major contributor with 20.1% of total sales, which is an increase from the previous 19.5%.
- USA (Production) sales made up 6.6%, which is an increase from the previous 6.1%.
- The Republic of Korea represented a notable 12.5% of sales, up from the previous 9.9%.

The overall consolidated sales amounted to 453.9 million euros, a decrease from the previous total of 467.3 million euros. This indicates a balanced contribution from both European and Asian markets to the company's total sales.
